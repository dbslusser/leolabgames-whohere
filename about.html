Who Here?™ is the ultimate party game for as many players as you have partiers (the more the better!). Included are 350 games cards describing interesting, whimsical, or revealing characteristics or scenarios that you match to the most applicable or deserving player. The first player to earn the target number of cards wins! Players want to earn cards, and can influence the judge with the best explanation why they match the card.



Who Here?™ is intended for mature audiences; discretion is advised.
Playing Who Here?™ can result in your party erupting in one or more of the following:
  • spontaneous limbo contests
  • everyone talking like a pirate
  • legendary paper airplane battles
  • mass marshmallow asphyxiation
Also your dog may win.
